To build a foundation wherein the students, the schools, and the patrons come together to function as “स्कूलिंग hub”.
Education, the process of facilitating learning, or the acquisition of knowledge, skills, values, morals, believes and habits are considered to be the highest form of worship. Education is very important as it makes an individual empowering. Self-help is easier for an educated person than one who is not educated. Thus, it becomes the moral right of every person to get educated. But still there exist many such sections of the society whose children are deprived of getting this elementary education from good schools due to lack of resources or knowledge. Hence, through this project, we have aimed at creating a platform wherein these kids can take benefit of getting education by studying in schools wherein they would get enhanced opportunities and excellent resources.
